About Us

NeuroViT is a transformer-enhanced AI framework designed for precise pituitary tumor detection using brain MRI scans. Our goal is to improve diagnostic accuracy and support safer and more reliable clinical decision-making.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance medical imaging through intelligent AI systems that enable accurate tumor detection and reliable segmentation. We strive to support early diagnosis, reduce diagnostic errors, and improve patient safety.

FAQ

What dataset was used to train NeuroViT?

NeuroViT was trained and evaluated on a curated brain MRI dataset containing both pituitary tumor and non-tumor images, with preprocessing and class balancing techniques applied to improve generalization.

Is NeuroViT ready for real-world clinical use?

NeuroViT shows promising research results. However, broader validation on multi-institutional datasets and regulatory approvals are required before full clinical deployment.

Does NeuroViT replace a radiologist?

No. NeuroViT is a decision-support tool designed to assist clinicians by providing AI-driven insights. Final diagnosis and treatment decisions remain under medical expertise.

Can NeuroViT detect small or irregular tumors?

Yes. By combining spatial precision with contextual modeling, NeuroViT is designed to identify subtle and irregular tumor patterns that may be challenging to detect using traditional methods.

Is my medical data safe when using systems like NeuroViT?

NeuroViT is designed as a research framework. In real-world deployment, such systems must follow strict medical data privacy and security standards to protect patient information.
